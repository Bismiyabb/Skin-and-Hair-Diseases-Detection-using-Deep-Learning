ABOUT


* Skin Disease(clinical images): This model can predict Atopic Dermatitis and Eczema and is suitable for normal users and Doctors.

* Skin Disease(Dermatoscopic images): This model can predict Basal Cell Carcinoma,Melanocytic Nevi and Melanoma.This model is preferable for doctors.

* Hair Disease: This model can predict Alopecia Areata,Folliculitis,Psoriasis,Seborrheic Dermatitis and Tinea Capitis

VISION

We predict different types of skin disease and hair disease. We are hoping for a community in which all people achieve their full potential for health and well-being across the lifespan. We work to be trusted by patients, a valued partner in the community, and creators of positive change.

MISSION


* To predict multiple skin and hair disease

* To reduce mortality rate

* Better Treatment targeting

* Reducing healthcare cost
